Core Features

Master the art of guild management in a world of perpetual darkness

👑

Guild Master Fantasy

You don't fight. You scout, train, and manage heroes who fight in strict, automated combat simulations. Your decisions before battle determine victory or defeat.

📚

Academy System

Scout unproven youth from three distinct regions, assign veteran Mentors, and guide Adepts through multi-year development cycles. Uncover hidden potential and shape the next generation of heroes.

⚔️

Deterministic Combat

Watch battles unfold in a 60 tick-per-second combat engine with 2x4 grid formations. Six damage types, eight hero classes, and deep ability synergies create endless strategic possibilities.

🔄

Cyclic Progression

Navigate recurring Cycles with unique global rules called Edicts that force you to constantly adapt your strategies. Each Cycle brings new challenges, rewards, and opportunities for your guild.

🌟

Hero Lifecycle

Heroes age from eager Adepts through their Prime years into eventual Decline and Retirement. Every hero's journey is unique, shaped by their battles, mentors, and your guidance.

🛡️

Unique Classes

From stalwart Gatekeepers to mystical Confessors, each class brings distinct abilities and roles to your formations. Master their synergies to overcome the darkness.
