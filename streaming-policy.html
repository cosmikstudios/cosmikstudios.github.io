The short version

Yes. You can stream Donor Zero. You can record it, edit it, post it, monetize it. Ads, subs, donations, Patreon, sponsorship, all good. No paperwork required.

Use logos and screenshots in your channel branding, thumbnails, overlays, and merch if you want. Free promotion is a gift, and we treat it like one. The only thing we ask is that you don't claim to be officially partnered with, endorsed by, or working for Cosmik Studios when you aren't.

The audio is safe

All music and audio in Donor Zero is original, composed for this game, and 100% owned by Cosmik Studios. There are no licensed tracks. There is no Content ID-flagged music. There is no need for a "streamer mode."

You can stream and upload with the in-game audio on, on any platform, without DMCA risk from us.

If you get a Content ID claim anyway

No entity has our permission to monetize, demonetize, or take down your videos based on Donor Zero's audio. If a claim is ever made on your video for our soundtrack or sound effects, it is not from us, and it is not authorized.

Please dispute the claim and cite this page as your authorization. If you need a written follow-up, email contact@cosmikstudios.dev and we'll help.

A few small things to avoid

  • Don't repackage and resell raw gameplay footage or cutscenes as a standalone product.
  • Don't post the soundtrack as a standalone music release. The score is part of the game; if you want to promote a track, please leave it inside a video about Donor Zero.
  • Don't claim to be officially partnered with, endorsed by, or working for Cosmik Studios when you aren't. Saying "I love this game" is great. Saying "I am Donor Zero's official partner" when you aren't is not.
  • Don't use Donor Zero or Cosmik Studios branding to promote unrelated products, NFTs, scams, or hate content.

If you want a soft favor

None of this is required. But if you'd like to make our day:

  • Drop the Steam page in your video description.
  • Tag @CosmikStudios on X when you post. We try to re-share streams and videos from our official account.

Reserved rights

This policy grants permission for the uses described above and nothing more. It is not a transfer of intellectual property, a trademark license for unrelated products, or an endorsement.

Cosmik Studios may update or revoke this policy at any time. Changes apply going forward and won't retroactively affect content you've already posted in good faith under the previous version.
