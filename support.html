Reporting Bugs

Found a bug in Darkest Exiles? Help us improve the game by reporting it!

In-App Bug Report

The easiest way to report a bug is directly within the game:

  • Long-press anywhere on the screen for 2 seconds to trigger the bug report gesture
  • This will open a bug report form with relevant diagnostic information already attached
  • Describe the issue and submit — it helps us fix problems faster!

Email Bug Report

Alternatively, you can email us with:

  • A description of what happened
  • Steps to reproduce the issue (if known)
  • Your device model and iOS version
  • Screenshots or screen recordings (if helpful)

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the closed beta?

Join our Discord server to receive a TestFlight invite. Once you're in the server, you'll find instructions on how to get access to the beta.

What is TestFlight?

TestFlight is Apple's official app for testing beta versions of iOS apps. It's free to download from the App Store. Once you accept a TestFlight invite, the beta version of Darkest Exiles will appear in your TestFlight app, where you can install and update it.

Will my beta progress carry over to the full release?

Most likely not. Beta versions are often reset before the official launch to ensure a fresh start.

Is my game progress saved to the cloud?

Currently, game progress is saved locally on your device. We recommend keeping regular iOS backups to protect your save data. Cloud sync may be added in a future update.

How do I disable analytics/telemetry?

Go to Settings in the game and toggle off the Analytics option. We use privacy-focused, anonymized analytics to improve the game, but you can opt out at any time.

The game is crashing or not loading. What should I do?

Try these steps: (1) Force-close the app and reopen it. (2) Restart your device. (3) Check for app updates in the App Store. (4) Ensure you have enough storage space. If the issue persists, please contact us with your device model and iOS version.

Will Darkest Exiles be available on Android?

We're currently focused on the iOS version. An Android release may be considered in the future based on demand and resources. Follow us for updates!

Response Time

We aim to respond to all support requests within 48 hours. During busy periods or after major updates, response times may be slightly longer.

Thank you for your patience and for playing Darkest Exiles!
